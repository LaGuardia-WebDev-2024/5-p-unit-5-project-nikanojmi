All About New Music

New Music is a singer and songwriter class at Lagaurdia high school, where musicians, singers and songwriters get together to workshop their musical crafts as an ensamble. This class is directed by Robert Apostle, who is a fantastic mentor, teacher, and pianist. He a bit cray cray but we love him.
